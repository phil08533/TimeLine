About

You own your data.

My Circle is a private photo & file sharing app with no backend. Every photo, file, and circle you create lives exclusively in your Google Drive — not on our servers, not in our database, not accessible to us.

When you share something with a circle, you're sharing a Drive folder directly with those people via Google's own permission system. When they view it, they're reading from your Drive. Delete it, and it's gone everywhere.

How it works

  • Circles and collections are folders in your Drive under mycircle/
  • Sharing = Google Drive's native share permissions — no middleman
  • Comments use the Google Drive Comments API, stored on the file itself
  • Likes are a reactions.json file inside each collection folder
  • Your friend list lives in mycircle/contacts/friends.json — readable only by you

Privacy

We request the drive scope to read and write files in your Drive. We only touch files inside the mycircle/ folder we create for you. OAuth tokens are stored in sessionStorage only — they are cleared when you close the tab. No telemetry, no analytics, no tracking of any kind.
