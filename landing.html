Everything you want. Nothing you don't.

◎

Circles

Create private groups — Family, Hiking Crew, Book Club. Share posts, photos, and files only with the people in each circle. You control membership.

📸

Albums

Curate collections by occasion — trips, birthdays, weddings. Invite contributors to add their own photos. Share the whole album with a circle or specific friends.

🌀

Stories

Ephemeral 24-hour stories with gradient rings. Your friends see your story right in their feed — no separate app needed.

💬

Comments & Reactions

React with 5 emoji types and leave comments on any post. Everything is stored via Google Drive — fully private, no third-party storage.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Friends

Friend requests, friend lists, and a chronological feed that only shows content from people you've added. Block or hide anyone.

🎨

Themes

Four visual styles (Minimal, Soft, Editorial, Brutalist) and five color palettes. Your choice persists across sessions. Make it yours.

🔒

PIN-protected Files

Hide specific files behind a PIN code right in your Google Drive. Only you can unlock them — the key never leaves your device.

☁️

Powered by Google Drive

15 GB free storage. No new account, no new password. Sign in with Google and My Circle creates a mycircle/ folder in your existing Drive.

∞

VoidScroll — built right in

An open video feed with no dopamine traps. Swipe through nature, science, history, and more — then share your favorites to your feed, circles, or albums with one tap.

Get started in 60 seconds

1

Sign in with Google

No new password. No new account. Just click the Google sign-in button — that's it.

2

Create your first Circle

Add a name, optional picture, and invite friends by email. They get access via Google Drive permissions.

3

Share photos & posts

Drop photos, videos, or text into the circle. Only members can see them — stored in your Drive, not ours.

4

React, comment, connect

Emoji reactions, comments, and stories. The full social experience — with none of the surveillance capitalism.
