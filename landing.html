My Circle is a private photo and file sharing app where everything lives in your Google Drive — not our servers. No algorithm, no ads, no one reading your posts.

Requires a free Google account. No signup form, no password, no credit card.

Everything you want. Nothing you don't.

◎

Circles

Create private groups — Family, Hiking Crew, Book Club. Share posts, photos, and files only with the people in each circle. You control membership.

📸

Albums

Curate collections by occasion — trips, birthdays, weddings. Invite contributors to add their own photos. Share the whole album with a circle or specific friends.

🌀

Stories

Ephemeral 24-hour stories with the Instagram-style gradient ring. Your friends see your story right in their feed — no separate app needed.

💬

Comments & Reactions

React with 5 emoji reactions and leave comments on any post. Comments are stored on the file itself via Google Drive — fully private, no third-party storage.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Friends

Friend requests, friend lists, and an easy-to-use feed that only shows content from people you've added. Block or hide users you don't want to see.

🎨

Themes

Four visual styles (Minimal, Soft, Editorial, Brutalist) and five color palettes. Default is the calming light-blue Soft + Slate. Make it yours.

🔒

PIN-protected Files

Hide specific files behind a PIN code right in your Google Drive. Only you can unlock them — the key never leaves your device.

☁️

Powered by Google Drive

15 GB free storage. No new account, no new password. Sign in with Google and My Circle creates a mycircle/ folder in your existing Drive.
